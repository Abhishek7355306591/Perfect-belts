About Us

"PERFECT" an organization to manufacture exclusive spare parts for Woven sacks TAPELINES & CHEESE,CIRCULAR LOOMS,PRINTING,LAMINATION,BCS & VALVOMATIC,NEWLONG & UNION SEWING MACHINES, AD STARKON & CONVERTEXUsed in the manufacture of pp Woven sacks was established in 1996 initially with heddle belts and grommet bands business because of its experience in the manufacture of nylon sandwitch belts(flat belts).it took some years to add more & more spare parts to expand our business at the behest of our established customers.Today we are manufacturing about 2500 different spare parts in various sizes and dimensions. We may be first among many to produce such a huge range of spare parts under one roof...

Our overseas business spans About 92 Countriesin Asia,South East, Middle East, Russia, Western & Eastern Europe,Africa, South & Central America. We have developed Spare parts for most modern and efficient FX & RX series looms from Austria

At present, these new highly productive looms are not introduced in india but whenever these are available, we shall be able to offer spare parts for these coveted machines also. It may be very interesting to note that most of these spare parts are manufactured under one roof, facilitating and ensuring utmost quality & exceptional speed of execution of your orders.

In india, we are supplying our spare parts to every woven sack loom & other machinery manufactures meeting their specialized specifications and other needs to their entire satisfaction.
